Word Drop

A Tetris-like falling letter game with rotating pieces and word matching in any direction.

Controls

Left / Right - move
Down - soft drop
Up - rotate
Space - hard drop
P - pause
R - restart
Rules:
- Each falling piece contains 4 letters.
- Rotate and place pieces to form words.
- Words are detected horizontally, vertically, and diagonally.
- Matching letters clear and the remaining letters fall downward.
- Chain clears are possible.
