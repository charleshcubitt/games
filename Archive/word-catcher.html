Controls

Left / Right - move active word lane

Rules:
- One letter falls at a time.
- Partial words 5-8 letters long move in from the screen edge at the bottom.
- Use the arrow keys to slide the word row left or right.
- Catch the falling letter in the correct blank spot to complete the word.
- Completed words disappear and new ones keep scrolling in.
