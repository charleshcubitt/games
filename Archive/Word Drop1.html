Word Drop

A Tetris-like falling letter game. Make horizontal words to clear them.

Controls

Left / Right - move
Down - soft drop
Up or Space - hard drop
P - pause
R - restart
Rules:
- Each falling block is one letter.
- When a row contains a valid word of 3 or more adjacent letters, those letters clear.
- Gravity pulls letters above downward after a clear.
- Chain clears are possible.
